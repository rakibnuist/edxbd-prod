# EduExpress China SEO Authority Plan

China is the proof engine: it must become the strongest Bangladesh-specific China education resource online. EduExpress presents itself as a guidance and application-support company — never as an exclusive embassy or scholarship authority. The Chinese Embassy in Bangladesh states it has NOT authorized any exclusive visa agency; never imply otherwise.

## Approved China offer wording (use verbatim on policy/commercial pages)

> "No file opening charge. No EduExpress service fee before China visa approval. Any university, embassy, medical, translation, courier, deposit or other third-party fee required earlier must be itemized in writing before the student proceeds."

Always distinguish EduExpress charges from third-party charges. Never write "All payment after visa" without the third-party fee explanation.

## Success-story template (every story must include)

1. Student name or approved display name; authentic photo with written consent
2. Academic profile, target subject, budget, constraints
3. Options considered and why the final university/program was selected
4. Offer/scholarship facts with sensitive data redacted
5. Application and visa timeline — NO visible passport number, date of birth, or visa identifier
6. Exact EduExpress service provided and what the student paid
7. Current student update after arrival, when available
8. Internal evidence ID and consent-expiry/revocation record

## China conversion path (design pages to move students along it)

Search guide → university/program comparison → Education Fit Assessment → ClearCost Sheet → application proof tracking → visa readiness → service fee after visa approval → pre-departure support → consent-based success story.

## Rules

- Official 2026/27 CSC scholarship notices and embassy visa notices are the source material; store source URL + verification date in CMS for every time-sensitive fact.
- MBBS content must lead with recognition/licensing caveats, not price.
- Never borrow China statistics for any other destination.
- Scholarships are an affordability factor assessed after education fit — China pages sell a better education decision, not scholarships.
- Bengali pages: translate fully and professionally before adding hreflang; no partial translations.
