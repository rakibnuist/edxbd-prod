# EduExpress Destination Page System

SEO plan for the 11 side destinations. Each country needs one pillar + at least five useful supporting pages before serious backlink outreach. Pages must be Bangladesh-specific, based on current official sources. Thin copied country pages must NOT be indexed.

## Page structure per destination — build in this order

| Page type | URL pattern | Content requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Pillar | /study-in-{country}-from-bangladesh | Education fit, entry, total cost, recognition, visa, intakes, realistic benefits/risks, service status |
| Cost | /{country}-study-cost-bangladesh | Full first-year + total-program cost in local currency and BDT; assumptions/date |
| Visa | /{country}-student-visa-bangladesh | Official process, funds, documents, interview/biometrics, refusal risks |
| Scholarship/funding | /{country}-scholarships-bangladesh | Funding as a subset of education options; renewal and conditions |
| Universities | /{country}-universities-bangladeshi-students | Verified shortlist + why each type of student may fit |
| Intakes/deadlines | /{country}-intakes-deadlines | Dynamic calendar with open/closed logic |
| Career/recognition | /{country}-degree-recognition-career | Qualification recognition, realistic progression; no job guarantee |
| Bengali guide | /bn/... | Only priority pages, fully and professionally translated; hreflang |

## Country-page module order (use exactly)

1. Breadcrumbs + service-status badge
2. H1 + 50-word direct value statement
3. Who this country fits — and who should reconsider
4. Education system and recognition
5. Program/university choices with evidence
6. Admission requirements for Bangladesh
7. Total cost and funding reality
8. Visa/residence process with official sources
9. Work rights and career pathways with caveats
10. Intakes/deadlines with automatic status
11. Comparison with two alternatives
12. FAQ with visible answers
13. Country reviewer, sources, last verified date
14. CTA: Education Fit Assessment

## Indexation rule for incomplete countries

A "Launching Soon" lead page may be indexable ONLY when it contains genuine service information, team ownership, launch status, a useful assessment offer, and enough original content. Empty country cards or copied generic pages: noindex or withhold entirely.

## Internal linking model

- Every supporting guide links to: its country pillar, 2 relevant university/program pages, 1 transparency/service page, 1 related guide
- Country pillars link to all priority cluster pages
- Success stories link to the actual university/program and the Better Education Standard
- Descriptive natural anchors — no exact-match keyword stuffing

## Launch prerequisites

Before publishing a country cluster, confirm the 8-item launch gate in the `eduexpress-seo-strategy` skill has passed, and check that country's guardrail in its destination matrix (jurisdiction language for Cyprus, medical licensing for Georgia, no "Schengen" selling for Croatia, etc.). Never display a success rate or testimonial from another destination.
