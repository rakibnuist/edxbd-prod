# EduExpress Evidence-First SEO Strategy

The master strategy skill. All other EduExpress skills (technical-seo, china-seo, destination-pages, content-governance, local-entity-seo, links-conversion-analytics) inherit the rules here.

## Core decision

EduExpress must NOT compete as another scholarship/"easy visa" consultancy. The market gap is **verified education advice, visible fee transparency, and written evidence** in a sector plagued by fraud, lost money, and misuse of student documents.

**Approved positioning (use verbatim):**

- Category position: **Bangladesh's Evidence-First Education Consultancy**
- Brand promise: **Better Education. Clear Costs. Written Proof.**
- Campaign line: **Compare Before You Commit.**
- China offer (approved wording): *"No file opening charge. No EduExpress service fee before China visa approval. Any university, embassy, medical, translation, courier, deposit or other third-party fee required earlier must be itemized in writing before the student proceeds."*

Why the wording matters: "All payment after visa" is only powerful when literally true. Third-party fees (application fees, deposits) may be required earlier — the website must distinguish EduExpress charges from third-party charges so the promise stays honest and defensible.

## Language rules (apply to ALL EduExpress copy)

| Use | Avoid |
|---|---|
| "We compare education quality, cost, recognition and career fit before recommending an option." | "Guaranteed admission" / "guaranteed visa" |
| "No EduExpress service fee before China visa approval." | "All payment after visa" without third-party fee explanation |
| "Scholarship options are assessed after education fit." | "We sell full scholarships" |
| "Current information verified on [date] from [source]." | Unattributed rankings, fees, or visa claims |
| "Launching service for [country] — assessment now available." | Borrowing China stats for a new destination |
| "Past performance does not guarantee an individual visa outcome." | "98% success guaranteed" |

**Management rule:** No destination may display a success rate, "partner" claim, guaranteed scholarship, easy-visa statement, or testimonial until the supporting record is stored in the CMS and approved by management.

## The four productized trust assets

1. **EduFit Decision Report** — 1-2 page comparison of at least 3 appropriate options, explaining why each is recommended or rejected.
2. **ClearCost Sheet** — written cost schedule separating university/embassy/third-party costs from EduExpress charges, with due dates, payment recipient, refund status.
3. **Application Proof Pack** — copies/references for submissions, receipts, offer records, deadline history, responsible staff member.
4. **China Visa-First Service Terms** — no file-opening charge, no EduExpress service fee before visa approval, with clear disclosure of earlier official fees.

## The Better Education Standard (6-point check)

1. **Recognition** — official registry/accreditor source, notes, date checked
2. **Academic fit** — program modules, entry requirements, counselor rationale
3. **Career value** — curriculum-to-career map; no guaranteed-job language
4. **Total financial reality** — tuition, living, official fees, EduExpress fee, currency assumptions
5. **Visa readiness** — checklist, gaps, official sources
6. **Student support & safety** — support scope, contacts, limits of responsibility

## Country launch gate — ALL 8 items required before launch

- [ ] Named country owner + trained backup counselor
- [ ] ≥5 verified institutions OR documented direct application route
- [ ] Official admission, visa, funds, work-rights, dependent info captured with source URLs
- [ ] Service scope, fee schedule, refund policy, responsibility matrix approved
- [ ] Country-specific SOP, CRM stages, document checklist, escalation process tested
- [ ] ≥1 expert-reviewed pillar page + 3 supporting guides ready
- [ ] No success story/performance claim unless it belongs to that destination with recorded consent
- [ ] Quarterly review owner + expiry date configured in CMS

## Decision rules for scaling (never violate)

- Don't add a country because a competitor has it; add it when EduExpress can verify and service it better.
- Don't increase content volume while pages are stale; fix accuracy first.
- Never use a China success statistic on another destination.
- Never accept payment without written purpose, recipient, milestone, refund status.
- Never publish student documents or identifiable visa pages.
- Don't chase backlinks until the linked page is genuinely useful and current.

## Final strategic instruction

Do not build the biggest-looking consultancy website. Build the most verifiable education decision platform in Bangladesh. China supplies the first proof. Transparent systems make the promise credible. New destinations expand only when the same standard can be delivered.
