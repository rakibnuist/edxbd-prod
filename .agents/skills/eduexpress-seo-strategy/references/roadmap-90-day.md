# 90-Day Implementation Roadmap

## Days 1-14
- **Developer:** SSR/SSG, metadata template, canonicals, redirects plan, analytics, privacy forms
- **SEO/content:** Crawl inventory; map keywords; identify thin/duplicate/stale pages
- **Management/counselors:** Approve USP, China payment wording, one source of company facts
- **Definition of done:** P0 technical blockers and claim rules signed off

## Days 15-30
- **Developer:** University unique IDs, status engine, sitemaps, schema, performance fixes, old-domain redirects
- **SEO/content:** Rewrite homepage, services, China pillar, fee/transparency and verification pages
- **Management:** Verify China evidence, fee exceptions, NAP, LinkedIn and profile facts
- **Definition of done:** New brand architecture live; duplicate risk controlled

## Days 31-60
- **Developer:** Country CMS, comparison modules, success-story model, secure uploads, CRM routing
- **SEO/content:** Publish 4 China guides, 5 top university pages, 2 case studies; Wave 1 briefs
- **Management:** Train country owners; approve UK/Hungary/Korea/Finland/Malaysia readiness
- **Definition of done:** China hub generating measurable leads; Wave 1 gate decisions made

## Days 61-90
- **Developer:** Launch qualified Wave 1 pillars, Bengali framework, dashboard, QA automation
- **SEO/content:** Complete remaining priority China content; first Wave 1 support pages; local landing page and GBP cadence
- **Management:** Review first KPI report, complaints/privacy process, launch status
- **Definition of done:** Stable, measurable platform with honest multi-country expansion

## Weekly operating cadence after launch

| Frequency | Task | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Daily | Lead response, status updates, review replies, policy-change alerts | Counselors / operations |
| Twice weekly | GBP post/photo + one social education explainer | Marketing |
| Weekly | One high-quality guide or major refresh; one technical crawl check | Content + SEO + developer |
| Biweekly | China success-story interview or student update | China team |
| Monthly | Partner/press outreach, KPI review, AI/entity visibility check, claim audit | Management + SEO |
| Quarterly | Country source revalidation, fee/deadline refresh, privacy/access audit, content pruning | Country owners + compliance |

## 12-Month roadmap

| Quarter | Objective | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Repair trust, own China foundations | Technical P0, USP, transparency system, China pillar, top guides, local SEO restart, Wave 1 readiness |
| Q2 | Build China authority, launch Wave 1 | Complete China cluster, Bengali pages, success stories, institutional links, Wave 1 country clusters, comparison tool |
| Q3 | Prove multi-country quality, launch Wave 2 | Original annual/data asset, stronger PR, Malta/Cyprus/Georgia where gates pass, subject clusters, refresh system |
| Q4 | Scale only what performs | Wave 3 gate review, program-level SEO, tools/calculators, content pruning, annual transparency/outcomes report, 2027 intake refresh |
